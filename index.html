Equipment

Vacuum Excavation

Introducing the Vermeer VX30-250 Vacuum Excavator. It allows our team to perform efficient non-destructive digging. It's portability makes it suitable for a wide range of environments while its reliability keeps us going job after job after job. Vaccuum Excavation prevents us from damaging assets making it perfect for digging around water, gas, electrical and communications.

Ground Penetrating Radar

The IDS Opera Duo is a ground penetrating radar that we use to find and map a variety of utilities. The Opera Duo simultaneously displays cables as well as metallic and non-metallic pipes together in realtime and its dual frequency antenna allows us to detect both deep and shallow targets. All this data is combined with its the information from its GPS to accurately survey the area.

Pathfinder Locating System

The Rycom Instruments 8869v3 Pathfinder Locating System allows us to identify utilities even in crowded environments. It operates on a range of frequencies so we can isolate individual lines and gives us information about whether cables are live and where pipes are blocked.
